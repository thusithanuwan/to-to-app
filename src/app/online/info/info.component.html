Online Payment Steps
  1. Request to map your property with online account
  2. Wait for approval from your Local Authority.
  3. Select your property and pay
 How to map my property with online account?
  1. Click On "Map My Property" Button below
  2. Fill Your Information
    • Owner Name : Name of you assessment registration
    • Assessment ID : Kform Number
    • Street Name
    • Last Paid Recipt Number
    • Description
  3. Click On Submit button
  4. Within two working days you will get the approval for the online payment. If not please contact us.
 How to pay ?
  1. Select your more info button
  2. Enter the value
  3. Click on pay
  4. Enter card details

    • Description : වෙනත් විස්තර ඇත්නම් ඇතුලත් කරන්න.
